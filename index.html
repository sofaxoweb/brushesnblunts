Why We Exist

Built in Love.
Growing in Legacy.

Brushes N' Blunts didn't start as a business plan. It started as a name — one that meant something deeply personal. The name was created by Aysha, and everything built since has been in her honor.

This brand is proof that creativity doesn't have a ceiling, that culture deserves its own canvas, and that art is one of the most powerful ways to heal, connect, and celebrate the people we love.

When you buy a kit, book a party, or send a bundle to a friend — you're not just buying paint. You're carrying a story forward. And that story matters.

— Brushes N' Blunts, for Aysha.
